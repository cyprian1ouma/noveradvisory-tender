Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services.

What is EGEP tender support?

EGEP (e-Government Procurement) tender support involves professional assistance in preparing, reviewing, and submitting tenders through government electronic procurement systems in Kenya.

How quickly can you review my tender documents?

Our standard review takes 2 working days upon receiving all required documents. Express review (24 hours) is available for urgent submissions.

Do you provide IFMIS account recovery?

Yes, we help recover IFMIS account credentials within 2 hours. Contact us with your business details for quick assistance.
